About Me

My name is Maanu, and I'm a sophomore at Emory University. I am pursuing a BS in Quantitative Studies with a linguistics concentration and a BA in Classical Philosophy. Professionally, I am interested in research about the relationship between data science and linguistics, specifically natural language processing, and how large language models are the future of technology. I'm also passionate about exploring digital humanities, and how computational methods can be used in literary analysis and linguistic pattern recognition.
