Current Projects

searchlegAI is a search engine which provides basic information about modern AI legal issues. The target audience for this site is all; while beginners can learn about interesting cases and their precedents, experts can brush up or get a quick summary about relevant cases. Taking inspiration from Wiktionary and popular search engines such as Google, the user interface is intended to be accessible to all, hoping to spread awareness about and encourage interest in the legal side of technology. Lingua Machina is an interactive text viewer for classical literature. It includes interesting features such as literary analysis through technology and even a generator which provides text in the style of the classics. The purpose of the site is to encourage viewers to explore the classics and recognize this field's importance in modern times. Click here to learn more!

Modern Metamorphoses is split into eight different sections. Each section provides an unadulterated view into the minds of our most cherished characters in famous Greek mythology. Based on the author’s original translations of Ovid’s Metamorphoses, Obalapuram takes through different perspectives on well-known tales and allows you to imagine yourself as another character–not as a reader. Modern Metamorphoses is a journey into the psyches of classic mythological figures--and a revival of the ancient stories that form our literature today. ***Likely to be published in late summer of 2025.
